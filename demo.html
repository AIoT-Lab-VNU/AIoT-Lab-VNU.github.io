Demo Request

Request a personalized demo of AIoT Lab VN's products

Thank you for your interest in AIoT Lab VN's products. We are excited to showcase our cutting-edge AIoT solutions through product demonstrations. To ensure a smooth and beneficial experience for all parties, please review our product demo policy below:

  • Requesting a Product Demo: To request a product demo, please visit our official website or contact our sales and support team. Provide essential details such as your name, company/organization, contact information, and a brief description of your specific requirements and use case.
  • Eligibility: Our product demos are primarily intended for potential clients, partners, and individuals interested in exploring our AIoT solutions for business or academic purposes.
  • Scheduling a Demo: Upon receiving your demo request, our team will promptly review your information and reach out to schedule the demo at a mutually convenient time. We strive to accommodate demo requests promptly, subject to availability.
  • Demo Format: Product demos can be conducted either remotely, through virtual meetings, or in-person at our designated location, depending on your preference and feasibility.
  • Confidentiality: AIoT Lab VN respects the confidentiality of your information and data. Any sensitive or proprietary information shared during the demo will be treated with the utmost confidentiality.
  • Feedback and Evaluation: We value your feedback on our product demo experience. Your inputs will help us continually improve our solutions and services.

Focused on innovation and collaboration, the lab drives advancements in technology, empowering sustainable growth and transformative solutions for the nation's future.
